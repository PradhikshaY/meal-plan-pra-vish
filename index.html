Weekend-Prepped

Snacks & Cravings.

3 options — 2 salty, 1 sweet. Prep all on Sunday. Each hits 100–200 kcal per serve with 5–12g protein.
🧂 Salty Fix #1
Masala Roasted Makhana
V: 30g (~1 large cup) — ~120 kcal, 4g protein
P: 25g — ~100 kcal, 3g protein

Toss in ½ tsp ghee, chaat masala, cumin, black pepper, pink salt. Roast at 180°C for 8–10 min. Cool completely before storing.
Batch roast 200g → stores 5–7 days in airtight jar.
🧂 Salty Fix #2
Sprouted Moong Chaat
V: ¾ cup — ~150 kcal, 10g protein, 7g fibre
P: ½ cup + 1 boiled egg — ~160 kcal, 14g protein

Sprouted moong + chopped onion + tomato + coriander + chaat masala + lemon. Dress fresh per serve.
Soak Friday night → sprouted by Sunday. Store plain in fridge.
🍫 Sweet Fix
Date & Nut Energy Balls
V: 2 balls — ~160 kcal, 5g protein, 4g fibre
P: 1–2 balls — ~80–160 kcal

Blend 10 Medjool dates + 3 tbsp almond butter + 2 tbsp flaxseed + 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa. Roll in desiccated coconut. Refrigerate 30 min.
Makes ~15 balls. Refrigerate up to 10 days. No baking, no added sugar.
